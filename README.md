## About Me

I am a Master's degree student in **Cybersecurity** at Sapienza University of Rome (expected graduation: October 2026), with a Bachelor's degree in **Computer Science** from the University of Perugia (104/110).

My academic and research focus lies at the intersection of **system security**, **kernel-level defense mechanisms**, and **network infrastructure**. My thesis project (**MCPsec**) involves the design and implementation of a proactive defense sandboxing system for Model Context Protocol (MCP) servers, leveraging **eBPF LSM** and **cgroups v2** to enforce fine-grained security policies at the kernel level.

I am passionate about both offensive and defensive security, and I actively strengthen my practical skills through CTF challenges on **HackTheBox** and **TryHackMe**.
